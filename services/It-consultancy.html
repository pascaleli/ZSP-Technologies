IT Consultancy and Managed Services

ZSP IT consultancy services offer strategic insights and expert recommendations to assist your organization in navigating the intricate technology landscape. Recognizing that each organization has unique requirements, we customize our services to align with your specific objectives.

Get Started

Infrastructure as a Service

Clients can now eliminate the need to invest in and manage their own physical infrastructure, such as servers, networking equipment, and storage devices. Instead, they can take advantage of virtualized resources that we host, which are readily accessible over the internet.

Platform as a Service

Our cloud computing model offers a comprehensive platform that allows our clients' technical teams to build, deploy, and manage applications without the difficulties of maintaining the underlying infrastructure. With our managed Platform as a Service (PaaS), developers and engineers can focus on programming and innovation, as we take care of the infrastructure, runtime environments, and essential development tools that support their creative efforts. This managed service simplifies the development process by providing our clients with a range of benefits, including:

  • Rapid Application Development
  • Optimized Scalability and Flexibility
  • Reduced Time to Market
  • Focus on Innovation
  • Robust Availability and Security

Software as a Service

Our proficiency in this managed service enables us to provide our clients' software applications via the internet, transforming their businesses from the old model of purchasing and installing software on individual computers or servers. Now, clients can access their software through a web browser that we host and manage centrally. This transition allows businesses and their customers to access solutions from anywhere, as long as they have an internet connection. Our managed service brings significant benefits to our clients' businesses, including:

  • Simplified Access to Solutions
  • Optimized Cost Efficiency
  • Streamlined Automatic Updates and Maintenance
  • Guaranteed Data Security and Dependability
  • Prioritize Core Business Activities

Internet-of-Things as a Service

Our service empowers clients to harness the advantages of IoT technologies without the burden of investing in the infrastructure and expertise required to build and maintain their own IoT platforms. We provide a comprehensive solution encompassing hardware, software, connectivity, and data analytics, enabling businesses to capitalize on the potential of IoT in a cost-effective and scalable manner. Our managed service transforms our clients' businesses by delivering the following benefits:

  • End-to-End Solution
  • Reduced Complexity and Cost
  • Data Analytics and Insights
  • Integration and Interoperability
  • Focus on Core Business

IT Consultancy as a Service

By operating as an IT Consultancy as a Service, we have positioned ourselves as a business enabler for our clients, delivering expert guidance, advice, and customized solutions to address their unique technology challenges and achieve their technology-related goals. This ultimately leads to a positive transformation of their business processes. As an IT consultancy service provider, we serve as a trusted advisory partner, helping clients make informed decisions, optimize their IT infrastructure, and maximize the value of their technology investments. Our managed service transforms our clients' businesses by offering the following benefits:

  • Strategic IT Guidance
  • Technology Assessment and Planning
  • Solution Design and Implementation
  • Change Management and Training
  • Ongoing Support and Advisory Services
